Classes of students



Little Dragons (Kung Fu for ages 6-11)

The Little Dragons kung fu classes not only teach valuable self defence skills but support childrens social interaction, focus and concentration skills, whilst keeping them fit and healthy. Children's lesson follow a traditional kung fu syllabus as set out by the British Kung Fu Association (BKFA). Our Little Dragons classes are fun and varied- all taught by a qualified primary school teacher. All children's classes also benefit from our 'mat-chat' program where we not only teach martial arts but a variety of life skills such as: anti bullying and taking ownership of your actions.
